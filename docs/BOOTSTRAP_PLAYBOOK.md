# Bootstrap Playbook: Launch & Scale with Free AI Tools

*A tested framework for building sellable offers with zero ad budget and minimal tools.*

---

## Core Prompts (Apply These Exactly)

### 1. List 10 Anonymous, No-Cost Business Ideas
Use free AI tools only. No paid software, no ad spend. Get actionable, launchable ideas tied to your skills or audience.

### 2. Turn [Skill] Into a Sellable Digital Product/Template
Identify the skill you already have. Package it for a specific niche. Define deliverable, audience, and pricing anchor.

### 3. Write Cold DM Copy That Converts
Value-first positioning. No neediness. Solve a real problem in 2-3 sentences. Include micro-offer or question that invites response.

### 4. 6-Day IG Content Schedule (No Video/Selfies)
Build trust without video production overhead. Use text, carousel posts, quotes, case studies, templates. One theme per day.

### 5. Simple Landing Page Copy (3 Core Elements)
- **Offer:** What you're selling, in one sentence.
- **Audience:** Who it's for and what pain point you solve.
- **Proof:** Social proof, results, or credibility marker (no fluff).

### 6. Onboarding + Delivery Flow (Notion/Google Forms)
Design the customer journey:
- Lead capture (form)
- Welcome sequence (email or Notion)
- Delivery mechanism (link, file, course structure)
- Feedback loop

### 7. Four Upsells (Minimal Extra Work)
Increase revenue per customer without scope creep:
- Done-for-you version of base offer
- Exclusive community/group access
- Monthly subscription/retainer variation
- Accountability/coaching add-on

---

## What Worked (Tested Pattern)

✅ **No ad budget** — relied on DMs, email, organic content  
✅ **No fancy tools** — Notion, Google Forms, email, social media only  
✅ **Consistent offers** — same pitch, refined over time  
✅ **Conversations over broadcasting** — 1:1 DMs > mass content  
✅ **Value-first positioning** — free resource, insight, or template first  

**Result:** Conversions through trust, not tactics.

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] Pick one skill or service to package
- [ ] Identify your niche / ideal customer
- [ ] Use Prompt #2 to define the product (deliverable, price, positioning)
- [ ] Use Prompt #5 to write your landing page copy
- [ ] Use Prompt #6 to design Notion onboarding + delivery
- [ ] Use Prompt #3 to write your outreach DM
- [ ] Use Prompt #4 to plan 6 days of content
- [ ] Use Prompt #7 to design upsells
- [ ] Launch: send 10 DMs, post 6-day sequence, track replies
- [ ] Iterate: refine pitch based on what people ask

---

## Why This Works

1. **Free tools remove friction** — no software bill, no setup delay
2. **Direct outreach beats ads** — conversations convert faster than impressions
3. **Value first builds trust** — give something useful before asking for money
4. **Simple flows close deals** — Notion + Google Forms = no code, no overhead
5. **Upsells multiply revenue** — same customer, different packages
6. **Consistency compounds** — small daily actions build momentum over weeks
