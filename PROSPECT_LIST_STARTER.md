## HOW TO FIND EMAIL ADDRESSES

### Method 1: LinkedIn (Free, 10 mins per person)
1. Search person's name + company
2. Click their profile
3. Look for email in "Contact Info"
4. If not there → check company website "Team" page
5. If not there → use Pattern: firstname@company.com

### Method 2: Hunter.io (Free for 25 searches/month)
1. Go to hunter.io
2. Enter company name
3. Get email patterns: john@company.com
4. Verify with LinkedIn match

### Method 3: RocketReach (Free trial)
1. Search company
2. Find employees
3. Get verified emails
4. Export to CSV

### Method 4: Apollo.io (50 free searches)
1. Enter company name
2. Get verified emails
3. Export to spreadsheet

### Method 5: Direct Company Websites
1. Visit company site
2. Look for "Contact" or "Team" page
3. Click individual profiles
4. Many show email addresses
